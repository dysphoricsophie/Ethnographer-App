Medical-style population composite. Each sheet shows a zoomed facial inset at upper left, a front body view, and a lateral profile. Male gluteofemoral deposition is allowed to appear when population-wide steatopygia is high and sexual dimorphism, endomorphy, and neoteny remain comparatively low. Figures are drawn with minimal underwear coverage for safe display while preserving anthropometric structure.
